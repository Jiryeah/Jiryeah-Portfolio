About Me

Hello! My name is Faissal Shabani and welcome to my portfolio. I'll assume that my name has you wondering where I might come from, so I'll assist you with that. I was born in the Democratic Republic of Congo, formlery Zaire, and I came to the states as a refugee due to the Genocide of '94 and the First and Second Congo wars.


My name comes from a king in Saudia Arabia who built and named the hospital that I was born in. Coincidentally, the name means "the stubborn one"; yes, I am extremely stubborn. Now, my love for technology mixed with a ferocious sense of sturbbornes has lead me to fall in love with programming. Programming allows me to create and use my persuit of perfection for a meaningful cause. Please allow this portfolio to illustrate some of my work and the passion that I have for development.
